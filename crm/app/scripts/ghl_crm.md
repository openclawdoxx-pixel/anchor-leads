Connects to your missed calls, your website chat, your reviews — everything in one place. $0 setup. $75 per booked job. First 3 free.

## Objections
### "I've tried CRMs, they're a pain"
This one I set up FOR you. Zero config. You open the app, see your leads, tap to call or text. If you hate it, toss it, you still keep everything.

### "I don't have time to learn software"
You already use a phone. If you can text, you can use this. That's the whole bar.

### "I'm fine with my spreadsheet"
Until you miss a follow-up on a $5k job because it got buried. One missed job pays for the entire system for a year.
