## Opener

## Discovery
- When somebody lands on your site at midnight with water coming out of a wall, what happens?
- If they can't reach you right then, are they calling the next plumber or waiting till morning?
- Would you want a system that greets them, gets their info, and books them — any hour, any day?

## Pitch
I add a chat widget to your site that handles inbound leads 24/7. Someone lands, gets a friendly "hey, what's going on?" — the AI asks the right questions, then texts you with the whole conversation and their contact info. You show up, you fix it, you get paid. Free setup. $75 per booked job. First 3 free.

## Objections
### "I don't want to deal with chat all day"
You don't. The AI handles it. You only see the summary — name, number, address, what's wrong. You don't talk to the widget, you talk to the customer.

### "My website barely gets traffic"
That's actually the point. Even a handful of visitors a month, if they book instead of leaving, is real money. And I can help get you more traffic too, but this is the first domino.

### "What if the AI messes up?"
It does the easy stuff — basic intake. Anything weird gets flagged and sent to you so you can take it manually. You have full control.
