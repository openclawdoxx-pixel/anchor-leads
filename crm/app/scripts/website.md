# Website Pitch — "The Leak-Proof Website"

## Opener
Hey [OWNER], [YOUR NAME] here — quick one, noticed your site and you don't really have a way for homeowners to book online right now. Got 30 seconds?

## Discovery
- How are most people finding you right now? Google? Word of mouth?
- When someone's got a burst pipe at 9pm and finds your site, what happens if they can't book?
- How many of those do you think you're losing to competitors every week?

## Pitch
Here's the deal — I build plumbers a full website, but it's not a brochure. It's a booking engine. Ranks locally, builds trust, and it's dead-simple for homeowners to book before they click on a competitor. The whole thing is $0 upfront. I only make money when the site actually books you a new paying job — flat $75 per booked job. Your first 3 are free. If I don't bring you real work, you pay nothing, ever. Want to see what it looks like?

## Objections
### "I already have a website"
Cool, what does it do? Does it book jobs or just show your phone number? Because the Google listing already shows your phone number — you're paying for a glorified business card. This one actively converts.

### "I'm too busy for this"
That's the whole point. You're too busy because you're under a sink, and every call that comes in while you're working slips through. This thing catches them without you lifting a finger.

### "How do I know it works?"
First 3 booked jobs are 100% free. If your average job is $400, that's $1,200 in your pocket before I earn a dime. You're playing with house money.

### "What if I want to cancel?"
Walk away anytime. You keep the website, the domain, everything. I don't believe in handcuffs.
